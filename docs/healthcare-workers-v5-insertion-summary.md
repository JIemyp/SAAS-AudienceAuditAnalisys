## Segment Profile Summary

**Who They Are:**
- Nurses, doctors, physical therapists, dietitians
- Age: 30-50
- Education: Bachelor's to Doctorate level
- Income: $61k-$208k/year

**What They Know:**
- Understand nutrition science deeply
- Familiar with gut-brain axis, microbiome, inflammation
- Can read and interpret clinical research
- Recognize pseudoscience vs evidence

**What They Need:**
- Sustained energy for 12-16 hour shifts
- Convenient nutrition despite chaotic schedules
- Evidence-based products they can trust
- Solutions that support recovery and prevent burnout

**How They Decide:**
- Research on PubMed/Google Scholar first
- Check third-party testing and certifications
- Ask peers in professional forums
- Trial with guarantee before committing
- Very high bar for proof and transparency

**What Wins Them:**
- ISO/HACCP certification (hospital-grade safety)
- Peer-reviewed research on microalgae
- RD/MD endorsements and testimonials
- Third-party testing publicly available
- Peer testimonials from other healthcare workers
- 60-day money-back guarantee
- Convenient frozen format (2 min prep)

**What Loses Them:**
- Misleading health claims ("cures", "detoxes")
- Proprietary blends (lack of transparency)
- Fake or unverifiable certifications
- Aggressive sales tactics
- Poor quality or contamination concerns
